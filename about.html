Who We Are

lumed (Association of Visionary Navigators) was founded in 2020 at the Euro-Mediterranean University of Fez with a vision to bridge the gap between technology, innovation, and practical applications.

Our club brings together passionate students from diverse backgrounds to collaborate on innovative projects that address real-world challenges through technological solutions.

What started as a small student initiative has grown into a vibrant community of over 200 members, working on cutting-edge research and community impact projects.

Our Mission

To foster interdisciplinary collaboration between technology, innovation, and various fields, creating innovative solutions for modern challenges while developing future leaders in technology.

Our Vision

To become a leading student-led organization in Africa that pioneers the integration of technology and innovation, creating impactful solutions that improve accessibility and quality of life.
