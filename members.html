Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about AVN membership

Who can join AVN?

AVN is open to all UEMF students from any discipline who are passionate about the intersection of technology and innovation. We welcome students from engineering, technology, AI, and other fields.

Is there a membership fee?

No, AVN membership is completely free for all UEMF students. We believe in making technology innovation accessible to everyone.

What time commitment is required?

The time commitment varies based on your level of involvement. You can participate in occasional events or take on more substantial roles in projects. We're flexible and understand academic commitments.

Do I need prior experience in technology?

No prior experience is required! We provide training, workshops, and mentorship to help you develop the skills needed to contribute to our projects.
