Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How can I join AVN?

You can join AVN by filling out the membership form on our Members page. All UEMF students are welcome to apply.

Do I need technical background to join?

No! We welcome students from all backgrounds. We provide training and workshops to help you get started.

When are your meetings?

We hold regular meetings every Wednesday at 6:00 PM. Check our Events page for the latest schedule.

Can organizations partner with AVN?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with organizations. Please contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.

How can I support AVN?

You can support AVN by joining our projects, participating in events, or sharing our initiatives with others.

Who can I contact for press inquiries?

For press and media inquiries, please email avn@uemf.org with "Press" in the subject line.
